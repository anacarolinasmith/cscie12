Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of Harvard Extension Technology Club?

Our mission is to be a platform where Harvard students and alumni can receive advice and network fellow students, alumni and professionals.

I am not enrolled in a technology field, but I am interested in HETC. Can I be a member?

Yes. All Harvard students and alumni are welcome to join. Our purpose is to inform past and future students of the vast resources available to them at Harvard University, and to share opportunities to contribute to the Harvard community.
